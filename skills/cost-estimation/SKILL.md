# Cost Estimation Workflow

## Objectives
- Estimate monthly costs by traffic tier
- Identify top cost driver and break-even risks
- Suggest optimization options without harming reliability

## Required Output
1. Cost assumptions
2. Prototype, early-traction, and growth tier estimates
3. Top cost driver per tier
4. Cost reduction options and tradeoffs
5. Budget risk alerts

## Validation Checks
- All estimates include assumptions
- At least one optimization recommendation per tier
- Cost-risk threshold is explicit
