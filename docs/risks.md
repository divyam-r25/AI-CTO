# Product and Technical Risks

## Top Risks
- Commodity positioning with low differentiation
- Cost spikes from external inference providers
- Latency volatility during growth periods
- Compliance exposure for sensitive text data

## Mitigation Strategy
- Define narrow wedge and clear unique value proposition
- Add caching, fallback providers, and usage caps
- Monitor latency SLO and queue depth continuously
- Introduce data classification and retention policy
